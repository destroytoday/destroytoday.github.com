Even though this is a college assignment from years ago, it's still one of my favorite projects to date. We were asked to make anything, but it had to consist of at least a thousand of the same object. Imagine the possibilities! I decided to build a 2 inch wall 12 feet long out of 2,000 sticks of Juicy Fruit. I stacked the gum like bricks, using water to adhere them all together.
