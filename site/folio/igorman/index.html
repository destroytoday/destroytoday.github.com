The I.Gorman Jewelers website was a freelance job I did through B. Creative Group in Baltimore. They hired me to develop the frontend using Flash. To set itself apart from typical jewelry websites, this one has a ‘hidden’ section, resembling a jewelry box, for saving and sharing items. I ended up using the site, as well as its myGorman feature, to find my fiance’s engagement ring.
